A Quick look at the types of triangles

 Triangles can be classified according to the length of their sides:
  • Equilateral triangle
    • It has all sides equal. All angles measure 60 degrees hence a regular polygon.
  • Isosceles triangle
    • It has two sides of equal length. The angles opposite to the two sides of the same length are also equal.
  • Scalene triangle
    • It has all sides of different length. Equivalently, it has all angles of different measure.
